```groovy
*  | +                                                                              + |  *
*  | ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++ |  *
*  | +++ tmux pane ++++++++++++++++++++++++++++ tmux pane +++++++++++++++++++++++++++ |  *
*  | +                                      +                                       + |  *
*  | +                                      +                                       + |  *
*  | +                                      +                                       + |  *

******************************************************************************************
```

- A tmux **session** is a collection of **windows**.
  - You can have multiple **sessions**.
  - Each **session** can have multiple **windows**.
- A tmux **window** is a collection of **panes**.
  - Each **window** can have multiple **panes**.
